Why Us?

At Cozy Cup, we want our guests to enjoy every steaming hot cup of coffee, which is why we only focus on high-quality bean mixes. We know that for many people, it's the coffee that gets you out of bed every morning. It can be a cherished habit to drink a cup of coffee with breakfast to prepare the body for a new day. We serve coffee only with fresh Arla milk as the great cup of coffee deserves only the best milk. Everything we do has an impact on the environment and our planet. We grow our coffee in the best possible way and take responsibility for the entire chain from bean to cup.
